Rules

How to play :

-This is a 5 letter Animal guessing game, where you make your next guess based on your performance in the previous one

Game Analysis:

A
- This means that you have the right letter in right place

A
- This means that this letter appears at someother place in the word

A
- This means that this letter is not in the word

You have 6 guesses to try and guess the Animal.

ROAR 'EM guesses!
